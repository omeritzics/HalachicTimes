# Halachic Times <img src="Halachic%20Times/app/images/ic_launcher-web.png?raw=true" width="50" height="50"/>
Halachic Prayer Times.

Times are calculated based on your location. Internet connection is being used to fetch your nearest address, otherwise the nearest country or time zone would be used.

## Daily Times
* Dawn (Alot HaShachar)
* Earliest tallis and tefillin (MiSheYakir)
* Sunrise (Hanetz HaChama)
* Latest Shema
* Latest morning prayers (Shacharit)
* Midday (Chatzot HaYom)
* Earliest afternoon prayers (Mincha Gedola)
* Afternoon prayers (Mincha Ketana)
* Plag HaMincha
* Sunset
* Twilight
* Nightfall
* Midnight (Chatzot HaLayla)
* Seasonal hour

## Weekly Times
* Candle lighting (before sunset on Fridays; Motzei Shabbat for Chag)
* Shabbat ending

## Monthly Times
* Molad (New Moon)
* Earliest Kiddush HaLevana
* Latest Kiddush HaLevana

## Annual Times
* Erev Pesach latest time to eat Chametz (same as latest Shacharit)
* Erev Pesach latest time to burn Chametz
* Number of Chanukah candles (before sunset on weekdays; Motzei Shabbat)
* Fast beginning and ending
* Sefirat HaOmer
* Festival ending

Click on a time to open a list with all of its known opinions.
